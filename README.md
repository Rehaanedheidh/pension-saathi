# Pension Saathi 🇮🇳

**A retirement planner for India's informal workers — from ₹42 a month.**

🔗 **Live app:** [YOUR-LINK]

## The problem

Over 90% of India's workforce is informal — drivers, street vendors,
domestic workers, gig workers, farm laborers. Unlike salaried employees,
they have no EPF, no employer pension, and no retirement safety net.
Most believe pensions "aren't for people like them."

The Government of India's **Atal Pension Yojana (APY)** offers a
guaranteed lifelong pension (₹1,000–₹5,000/month after 60) for
contributions as small as ₹42/month — but awareness is low and the
official contribution charts are hard to understand.

## What Pension Saathi does

- **Two ways to plan:** pick a pension goal and see the exact monthly
  cost, or enter what you can spare per day and see the best pension
  you can lock in at your age
- **Makes it concrete:** every contribution is translated into
  "cups of chai per day" (e.g. ₹8/day ≈ 1 chai)
- **Shows the payoff:** total contributed until 60 vs. guaranteed
  pension received by 80, plus the ₹1.7–8.5 lakh lump sum returned
  to family
- **Three futures:** the same savings kept as cash at home vs. a
  savings account vs. APY
- **Eligibility checker:** four simple questions, then plain-language
  enrollment steps
- **Hindi + English**, mobile-first, works offline

## How it works

All contribution figures come from the official PFRDA APY contribution
chart. The comparison uses a ~3.5% savings account rate and pension
received to age 80 (20 years) plus the scheme's corpus returned to the
nominee. Built as a single self-contained HTML file — vanilla
JavaScript, no frameworks, no dependencies — so it loads fast on
low-end phones and can be shared as a file over WhatsApp.

## Why I built it

I'm an IB student interested in business and finance. I wanted to
build something where financial math creates real access — not another
stock tracker. Retirement planning tools assume you have a financial
advisor and a 401(k)-style account; the people who most need planning
help have neither. Pension Saathi is my attempt to close that gap for
one specific, well-designed government scheme.

## Disclaimer

Estimates are based on the official APY chart. Pension amounts are
